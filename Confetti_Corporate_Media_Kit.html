The Platform

What Is Confetti?

An AI-powered lifestyle concierge that transforms how people discover and experience dining, nightlife, and city adventures worldwide.

Not a Listing. A Concierge.

Confetti doesn't show users 400 restaurants and wish them luck. It asks them what vibe they're feeling — Date Night, Rooftop Energy, Crew Outing, Family, Surprise Me — and builds a complete, bookable, multi-stop itinerary tailored to them.

Think of it as the difference between a phone book and a personal assistant. Your venue doesn't just get listed — it gets recommended by AI to the right person at the right moment.

Every itinerary is styled as a "Boarding Pass" — Departure, Layovers, Destination — making each outing feel like an adventure. Users earn Confetti points and unlock badges for completing itineraries, driving repeat engagement.
