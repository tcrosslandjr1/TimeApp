# Confetti — Business Model & Revenue Strategy

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** May 10, 2026 | **Target:** 1M subscribers in 18 months

---

## Executive Summary

Confetti is an AI-powered lifestyle concierge that helps users discover dining, nightlife, and experiences through mood-based recommendations, multi-stop itineraries, and group planning. Revenue comes from two sides: consumer subscriptions and business advertising credits (called "Boost Credits"). The model is designed for rapid viral growth through a generous free tier, with monetization scaling through B2B credit purchases.

---

## Consumer Pricing

### Confetti Free — $0/month
- 3 Confetti itineraries per month
- AI chat concierge
- Mood-based venue discovery
- Group planning (Crew mode)
- Earn Confetti rewards on check-ins
- Redeem coupons at partner venues

### Confetti Black — $4.99/month
- Unlimited Confetti itineraries
- 2x Confetti multiplier on all check-ins
- **$10/month outing credit** — real dollars toward your bill at any partner venue
- **3 prime reservations/month** — skip the wait at new & hot spots before anyone else
- Priority AI recommendations
- Early access to venue openings
- Exclusive Black-only experiences
- Skip-the-line at partner venues
- No ads — ever
- Black-tier badge on profile

### Why $4.99
- Below the "impulse buy" threshold — converts at 3-5x vs $9.99
- Comparable to Spotify/Apple Music mental model for "unlimited lifestyle"
- The $10 outing credit makes it feel like you're getting *paid* to subscribe — "I spend $5 but get $10 back when I go out"
- Prime reservations create FOMO and exclusivity — can't get that table without Black
- At 1M total users with 9% conversion = 90,000 Black subscribers = $449,100/mo ARR from consumers alone
- The outing credit is funded by partner venues (they give Confetti a negotiated discount, Confetti passes it through as a credit) — net cost to Confetti is ~$2-3/user/month, well under the $4.99 subscription
- The free tier is deliberately generous to drive viral adoption; the paywall is on *perks* and *access*, not features

## Business Pricing (Boost Credits)

Businesses purchase credits to increase visibility in Confetti's AI recommendations. Boosted venues only appear when taste-matched — users never see irrelevant ads.

### Tier Structure

| Tier | Monthly | Credits/mo | Max Campaigns | Analytics | Group Plan Placement | Dedicated Rep |
|------|---------|-----------|---------------|-----------|---------------------|---------------|
| Spotlight | $99 | 500 | 2 | Basic | No | No |
| Boost | $299 | 2,000 | 5 | Detailed | Yes | No |
| Partner | $799 | 6,000 | 15 | Premium | Yes | Yes |
| Enterprise | $2,500+ | 20,000+ | Unlimited | Enterprise | Yes | Yes |

### How Credits Are Spent

| Action | Credit Cost | Description |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Impression | 1 credit | Venue appears in user's AI recommendation feed |
| Click-through | 3 credits | User taps to view venue detail |
| Group Plan Inclusion | 5 credits | Venue placed in a Crew group plan |
| Featured Placement | 10 credits | Top-of-feed highlight with boost badge |
| Push Notification | 15 credits | Direct notification to taste-matched users nearby |

### Why Businesses Pay
- Confetti users are high-intent: they're actively planning to go out *tonight* or *this weekend*
- Taste-matching means zero wasted impressions — every view is a qualified lead
- Coupons + Confetti create guaranteed foot traffic, not just awareness
- Analytics show real attribution: impression → click → check-in → redemption

## Confetti Rewards System

Confetti is the app's reward currency. Users earn it through engagement and spend it on in-app perks.

### Earning Confetti

| Action | Free Tier | Black Tier |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| Regular check-in | 50 | 100 |
| First visit to a venue | 100 | 200 |
| Coupon redemption | 25 | 50 |
| Complete group plan | 200 | 400 |
| Refer a friend who joins | 500 | 1,000 |

### Spending Confetti (Future)
- 1,000 Confetti = unlock exclusive venue experiences
- 2,500 Confetti = priority reservation access
- 5,000 Confetti = free Confetti Black month
- 10,000 Confetti = VIP event invitations

### Economics
- Confetti costs Confetti nothing to issue — it's a loyalty mechanic
- The multiplier on Black creates an upgrade incentive ("your check-ins would've been worth 2x...")
- Redemption for a free Black month costs $4.99 in foregone revenue but retains a user who might churn

## City Expansion Strategy

### Launch Order
1. **Washington DC / DMV** — home market, deep local knowledge
2. **Atlanta** — massive dining/nightlife, strong social culture
3. **Miami** — year-round nightlife, tourism base
4. **New York** — density + prestige, proves scalability
5. **Los Angeles** — lifestyle capital, influencer amplification

### Per-City Playbook
1. Seed 50 partner venues with free Spotlight credits (90-day trial)
2. Launch with 500 beta users through Instagram/TikTok geo-targeted
3. Run "First 100 Check-ins" Confetti bonus event
4. Convert 10% of seeded businesses to paid within 60 days
5. Open city to general public once 20+ venues are active

### City Economics
- Each city reaches self-sustaining at ~30,000 users + 100 businesses
- Breakeven per city: ~$45,000/mo revenue vs ~$20,000/mo ops cost
- Target: 12 cities by month 18

## Viral Growth Mechanics

### Built-in Viral Loops
1. **Crew Mode:** Users invite friends to group plans → each invite is an app install
2. **Boarding Pass Share:** Users share their itinerary as a visual "boarding pass" on social → drives curiosity installs
3. **Confetti Referral Bonus:** 500 Confetti per friend who joins and checks in → creates referral flywheel
4. **Coupon Sharing:** "I just got 20% off at [venue]" social proof → attracts deal-seekers

### Estimated Viral Coefficient
- Average Crew size: 4 people → 3 invites per group creation
- 30% of groups are created by new users in their first week
- 40% of invited users install the app
- K-factor: ~1.2 (each user brings 1.2 new users on average)
- This means organic growth exceeds paid acquisition needs after month 6

## Competitive Moat

1. **AI taste-matching is the ad product** — traditional restaurant apps show everyone the same ads. Confetti's boost only surfaces to users who would actually like the venue. Higher conversion = businesses get better ROI = they spend more.

2. **Confetti creates lock-in** — users accumulate rewards they don't want to abandon. The more they use Confetti, the more valuable their account becomes.

3. **Group planning is viral by design** — every Crew plan is an acquisition event. No competitor has social-first discovery + AI recommendations + gamified rewards in one product.

4. **Two-sided network effect** — more users → more valuable to businesses → more coupons/rewards → more users. This flywheel accelerates in each city.

## Summary

Confetti monetizes through a freemium consumer model ($4.99/mo Black tier) and a B2B credit system where businesses pay $99-$2,500+/mo for taste-matched visibility. The Confetti reward system bridges both sides: businesses fund the rewards through credits, users earn and spend them through engagement, and Confetti captures margin on every transaction. At 1M users, projected ARR is ~$23M with a path to $50M+ as city expansion and enterprise deals scale.
