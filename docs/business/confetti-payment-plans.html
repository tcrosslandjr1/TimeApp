Billing policies.

What you need to know
01
No Long-Term Contracts. Monthly plans can be canceled at any time, effective at the end of the current billing period. No cancellation fees.
02
Quarterly & Annual Prepayment. Discounted plans are billed in advance. Early termination of prepaid plans will result in a prorated refund minus the monthly rate difference for months used.
03
Auto-Renewal. All plans auto-renew at the end of each billing cycle. You'll receive an email reminder 7 days before renewal.
04
Payment Methods. We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and ACH bank transfers. Payment information is securely processed via Stripe.
05
Late Payments. Invoices unpaid after 10 days accrue 1.5% monthly interest. After 30 days, the Venue Card may be paused until payment is received.
06
Boost Credits. Included monthly credits roll over for up to 90 days. Purchased add-on credits never expire. Credits are non-refundable and non-transferable.
07
Tier Upgrades. Upgrade at any time — we'll prorate the difference for the remainder of your billing period. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal date.
08
Taxes. All prices are exclusive of applicable sales tax, VAT, or other taxes, which will be added to your invoice as required by law.
